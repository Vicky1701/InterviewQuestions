# DSA Daily Study Plan - Pattern-Wise Question Distribution

## Study Schedule

- **Daily Commitment**: 2 problems √ó 30 minutes = 1 hour
- **Per Problem**: 20 min solving + 10 min solution review (if needed)
- **Revision**: Every 3 days, revisit previously solved problems

## Pattern-Wise Question Distribution (Total: ~150-200 questions)

### 1. **Sliding Window/Two Pointers** (15-20 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Medium
- **Key Problems**: Maximum sum subarray, longest substring without repeating chars, container with most water
- **Time Estimate**: 8-10 days

### 2. **Array/String Manipulation** (20-25 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Rotate array, merge intervals, group anagrams, product of array except self
- **Time Estimate**: 10-13 days

### 3. **Binary Search** (15-18 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Search in rotated array, find peak element, median of two sorted arrays
- **Time Estimate**: 8-9 days

### 4. **Sorting & Searching** (12-15 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Medium
- **Key Problems**: Quick sort, merge sort, counting sort, search in 2D matrix
- **Time Estimate**: 6-8 days

### 5. **Hash Map/Hash Set** (15-18 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Medium
- **Key Problems**: Two sum, subarray sum equals k, longest consecutive sequence
- **Time Estimate**: 8-9 days

### 6. **Stack & Queue** (12-15 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Valid parentheses, daily temperatures, implement queue using stacks
- **Time Estimate**: 6-8 days

### 7. **Linked List** (15-18 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Reverse linked list, detect cycle, merge two sorted lists
- **Time Estimate**: 8-9 days

### 8. **Trees (Binary Tree/BST)** (20-25 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Tree traversals, validate BST, lowest common ancestor
- **Time Estimate**: 10-13 days

### 9. **Graph Algorithms** (15-20 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium to Hard
- **Key Problems**: DFS/BFS, number of islands, course schedule, shortest path
- **Time Estimate**: 8-10 days

### 10. **Dynamic Programming** (20-25 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Fibonacci, coin change, longest increasing subsequence, edit distance
- **Time Estimate**: 10-13 days

### 11. **Backtracking** (10-15 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium to Hard
- **Key Problems**: N-Queens, generate parentheses, word search, subset generation
- **Time Estimate**: 5-8 days

### 12. **Greedy Algorithms** (10-12 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Medium
- **Key Problems**: Activity selection, fractional knapsack, minimum spanning tree
- **Time Estimate**: 5-6 days

### 13. **Heap/Priority Queue** (8-12 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Top K elements, merge K sorted lists, median from data stream
- **Time Estimate**: 4-6 days

### 14. **Trie (Prefix Tree)** (6-8 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Key Problems**: Implement trie, word search II, longest word in dictionary
- **Time Estimate**: 3-4 days

### 15. **Union-Find (Disjoint Set)** (6-8 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Number of connected components, accounts merge
- **Time Estimate**: 3-4 days

### 16. **Bit Manipulation** (8-10 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Medium
- **Key Problems**: Single number, counting bits, power of two
- **Time Estimate**: 4-5 days

### 17. **Math & Geometry** (8-10 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Easy to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Happy number, spiral matrix, rotate image
- **Time Estimate**: 4-5 days

### 18. **Divide & Conquer** (6-8 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Medium to Hard
- **Key Problems**: Merge sort, quick sort, closest pair of points
- **Time Estimate**: 3-4 days

### 19. **Advanced Topics** (5-8 questions)

- **Difficulty**: Hard
- **Key Problems**: Segment trees, fenwick tree, advanced graph algorithms
- **Time Estimate**: 3-4 days
